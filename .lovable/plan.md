

# DERMAI – Skin Lesion Detection Landing Page

A comprehensive, friendly, and approachable landing page for DERMAI with warm colors, rounded elements, and an interactive demo section.

---

## 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo + "DERMAI" branding
- Links to page sections: How It Works, Features, About, Pricing, FAQ, Contact
- CTA button: "Try It Free"

## 2. Hero Section
- Headline: "Your Skin Health, Simplified"
- Subheading explaining AI-powered skin lesion detection from a photo
- Primary CTA button to scroll to the demo section
- Friendly illustration/graphic of skin analysis concept

## 3. How It Works
- 3-step visual flow with icons:
  1. **Upload** – Take or upload a photo of a skin lesion
  2. **Analyze** – AI processes the image using advanced detection
  3. **Results** – Get insights and recommended next steps

## 4. Interactive Demo Section
- Drag-and-drop image upload area
- Users can upload a skin image and see a **mocked** analysis result (simulated confidence scores, lesion type classification, risk level indicator)
- Friendly disclaimer that this is a demo and not medical advice

## 5. Features Section
- Cards highlighting key capabilities:
  - AI-Powered Detection
  - Fast Results (seconds)
  - Privacy-First (images not stored)
  - Multiple Lesion Types Supported
  - Easy-to-Understand Reports

## 6. About / Team Section
- Brief mission statement about making dermatology accessible
- Placeholder team member cards with roles

## 7. Pricing Section
- 3-tier pricing cards (Free, Pro, Enterprise) with feature comparison
- CTA buttons for each tier

## 8. Testimonials
- Carousel or grid of user testimonial cards with avatar, name, quote

## 9. FAQ Section
- Accordion-style frequently asked questions (accuracy, privacy, supported conditions, etc.)

## 10. Contact / Footer CTA
- Final call-to-action with email signup or contact form
- Footer with social links, legal links, copyright

---

## Design Direction
- **Warm, approachable palette**: soft coral/peach accent, light backgrounds, gentle gradients
- **Rounded corners** and soft shadows throughout
- **Friendly illustrations** and icons
- **Smooth scroll** navigation between sections
- Fully **responsive** for mobile and desktop
